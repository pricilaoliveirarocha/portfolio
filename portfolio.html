WHO IS SHE?

Meet Pricila, a self-taught Software Developer from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. At 23 years old, she is currently working as an Intern at the State Attorney General’s Office of Rio de Janeiro. At the end of 2021, she felt the need for change and started learning JavaScript before her regular work hours, marking her entry into the IT field. It didn’t take long for JavaScript to capture her interest, leading her to take a leap of faith in March 2022, when she left her administrative back-office job to focus entirely on learning, starting her first internship in the field. Since then, she has been enhancing her skills in various languages and frameworks.
